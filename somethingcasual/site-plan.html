Content

Home page

People want to learn more about things like art for the real world and cutting-edge technology but rarely pay attention to the industry and art behind even the greatest games in history. Why? Video games have long denoted a history of awkward, reclusive, and sometimes troublesome players who are often viewed as introverted and unhelpful to society. Here a different picture is painted to light the real purpose in community these players have in bringing more forms of art to digital museums in almost every living room.

Images for the Home page

What Exactly Does the Gaming Industry Have to Offer The Rest of the World?

When people see the first home page of my website, they will get the appropriate perspective on the gaming community by showcasing the impressive and helpful technologies made possible. Audience viewers can be presented with the abstract perspectives granted by the stories some of our favorite fictional characters live in and the non fiction stories that would make you chuckle. Most importantly, highlighting the increasingly diverse and popular player-bases around the world is the common goal of almost all development teams in the industry, each with their own take on the focus to end borders and create communities.

Images for the Page 2

The Logistics and Help Needed Associated with Video Games.

The interactive exercises and fact givers will help peak interest in the already rich history and impact of the video game world. Everybody already has a relationship of some kind or another with video games, which I can use to get past the unnecessary greetings from one gamer to a non-gamer and get straight to the facts and impressive timeline of the video game industry. People already will be looking for information I offer anyway due to the industry's already impressive feat of gaining a revenue of more than twice Hollywood and the music industries combined! Surely, the future is turning its gaze more attentively to video games.
